SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Northwind Analytics Pvt Ltd
("Provider") and Solstice Retail Group ("Client"), effective as of January 15, 2026.

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

Provider shall deliver a customer analytics dashboard, including monthly churn prediction
reports and quarterly business reviews. Provider shall also maintain 99.5% uptime for the
hosted dashboard platform, measured monthly.

2. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement shall remain in effect for twelve (12) months from the effective date, and
shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party
provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the end of the
then-current term. Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach if such
breach remains uncured for thirty (30) days after written notice.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT

Client shall pay Provider a monthly fee of $18,500, due within fifteen (15) days of
invoice date. Late payments shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month. Provider reserves
the right to suspend services if payment is more than thirty (30) days overdue.

4. DATA OWNERSHIP AND CONFIDENTIALITY

All Client data processed under this Agreement remains the exclusive property of Client.
Provider shall not use Client data for any purpose outside the scope of this Agreement,
and shall implement industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit. Both
parties agree to keep the terms of this Agreement confidential for a period of three (3)
years following termination.

5. LIABILITY

Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by
Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. Neither party shall be liable for
indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

6. SERVICE LEVEL PENALTIES

If Provider's uptime falls below 99.5% in any calendar month, Client shall be entitled to
a service credit of 5% of that month's fee for each full percentage point of downtime
below the threshold, up to a maximum credit of 25% of the monthly fee.

7. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to
its conflict of laws principles.
